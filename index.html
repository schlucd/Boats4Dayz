A-boat Boats4Dayz

Boats4Dayz was first envisioned through a class project. We wanted to create an honest service that connects customers to customers by allowing boat owners with unused boats to rent them to people who don’t have the means to own one themselves.

It is our goal to make the Minnesota pastime of relaxing on the lake available to everyone. After all, with 10,000 lakes, there’s enough room to go around. We strive to make Boats4Dayz as secure, friendly and easy to use as we can and welcome feedback.
